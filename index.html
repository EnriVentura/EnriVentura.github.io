Ciao :)

I'm a PostDoc at Bocconi Institute of Data Science and Analytics (BIDSA) in Milan, Italy. Previously I've been a joint PhD student in Theoretical Physics at La Sapienza in Rome and École Normale Supérieure in Paris, under the supervision of Profs. Francesco Zamponi and Giancarlo Ruocco. My research investigates the statistical mechanics of disordered systems and its multidisciplinary applications.

At the moment I'm working at the interface between artificial intelligence and neuroscience. Specifically, I'm interested in biologically plausible forms of neuro-computing that bridge different aspects of learning: classification, memorization, and data-generation. I've been working on Perceptron models, Attractor Neural Networks and Energy-based generative models such as Boltzmann Machines and Diffusion Models.

Physics aside I'm passionate into Music, and Activism !
